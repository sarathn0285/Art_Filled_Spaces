Meet The Artist

S.N.T

Sarath is whimsical. She likes weird things like worm farms and edamame. She plays with lots of different art forms, her favorites being ceramics and mokuhanga. She's worked in dark rooms, in the woods, with fire, with vintage cameras, in swimming pools, in moving cars, and even in her own home. A native New Yorker as well as a black, queer, woman; Sarath has used her street knowledge and her Dominican heritage to create projects that delve into all of the things she cares about. Explore at your own risk.
